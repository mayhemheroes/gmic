  • for GIMP 2.8:

    • Windows: installer or .zip archive
      • Important installation instructions for GIMP 2.8 on Windows!

      The Windows version of the plug-in for GIMP 2.8 comes with a bunch of .DLL files that may be incompatible with those provided with your installation of GIMP. To avoid possible DLL conflicts, our installer thus copies all his files into a new subfolder, located in your user's plug-in directory, You have then to explicitly add this subfolder as a recognized plug-in folder in the GIMP preferences to make sure GIMP can detect and run the plug-in.

      Please follow the steps described in this tutorial video if you're not sure about the way to do this properly.
